FAQ

Questions We Hear All the Time

Can't find what you're looking for? Our team is happy to help.

Do I need coding skills to use Agile AI Systems?

+

Not at all. Agile AI is a fully no-code platform. You describe what you want your agent to do, upload your documents, configure your channels, and deploy — all from a visual interface. No engineering team needed.

Does your AI understand Gulf Arabic dialects?

+

Yes — this is our core differentiator. Our NLP models are trained specifically on Gulf Arabic, including Kuwaiti, Saudi, and Emirati dialects. The AI detects the dialect automatically and responds in the same style.

Is my data stored locally in the Gulf?

+

Yes. We offer local hosting options in Kuwait and Saudi Arabia for data sovereignty compliance. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit, with full PDPL and GDPR alignment.

How long does integration take?

+

Most businesses go live within 3-5 days. Simple chatbot deployments can be done in hours. Complex ERP integrations with RPA typically take 1-2 weeks. We handle all the technical work.

Can you integrate with our existing ERP and accounting software?

+

Absolutely. We integrate with all major ERP systems (SAP, Oracle, Odoo), accounting software (QuickBooks, Xero, local Gulf systems), CRMs (Salesforce, HubSpot), and banking APIs including KNET and Benefit.
